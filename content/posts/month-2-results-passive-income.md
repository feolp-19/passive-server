## The Storj Story

[Storj](/storj-node-setup-guide/) is the slowest earner — but the most interesting one to watch.

This month I was paid out **$0.42** with another **$1.26 held in escrow**. Storj holds 75% of all node earnings during the first 9 months as a deposit, so the real payout right now is intentionally tiny.

The drive itself tells the bigger story:

### Storj Drive Status

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 2 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Used space | 242 MB | **1.58 TB** |
| Free space | 3.06 TB | 1.72 TB |
| Vetting status | In progress | 4 of 4 satellites vetted |
| Drive utilization | <1% | **45%** |

In one month the node went from nearly empty to **almost half full**. That's huge. Storj rewards both stored data (held long-term) and egress traffic (when users download what's on your node).

Once the **9-month hold period ends in October 2026**, the held earnings release and monthly payouts jump significantly. The current $1.26 hold is a preview of what's compounding in the background.
