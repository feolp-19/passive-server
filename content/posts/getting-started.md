## What is Passive Income from Your Computer?

Passive income means earning money with minimal ongoing effort. With bandwidth sharing and storage rental apps, you can earn money by:

1. **Sharing your unused internet bandwidth** - Companies use it for market research, price comparison, and content delivery
2. **Renting out your extra storage** - Become part of a decentralized cloud storage network

## What You'll Need

- **Any computer** - Old laptop, desktop, Mac Mini, or even a Raspberry Pi
- **Internet connection** - The faster, the better (but even slow connections work)
- **Extra storage** (optional) - For apps like Storj

### Step 2: Install Your OS

I recommend **Linux Mint XFCE** because:
- Lightweight (low resource usage = more for earning apps)
- Stable (runs 24/7 without issues)
- Free

### Step 3: Install the Apps

Start with one or two apps and expand from there. I recommend starting with:

1. **Grass** - Highest earnings, easiest setup
2. **Honeygain** - Most established, reliable payments

### Step 4: Let It Run

Once set up, just let your computer run! Check in weekly to make sure everything is working.
