## The Grass Explosion

[Grass](/grass-passive-income-guide/) went from ~40K to ~105K points in a single month.

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |

| Points earned | 30,470 | 40,390 | ~105,000 |

| Lifetime total | 30,470 | 70,860 | **175,380** |
| Est. USD value | ~$20 | ~$27 | **~$70** |

What changed? Three things:

1. **Better uptime.** The Grass desktop app is running more consistently via `systemd` and `Xvfb`. More hours online = more points.
2. **Network growth.** Grass rewards increase as the network matures and attracts more data buyers.
3. **Compounding reputation.** Longer-running nodes tend to receive more tasks.

At 7,000 points per day, Grass alone projects to **$70-100/month** — from one app, on one machine.

The catch remains: Grass pays in $GRASS tokens on Solana. The USD value depends on token price. I'm holding, not selling.
